FAQ

+ Q1. What services does GulfBay Logistics offer?
A. GulfBay Logistics, a leading freight forwarding company in Dubai, offers comprehensive logistics solutions across the UAE and GCC. Our services include air freight, sea freight, land transportation, 3PL logistics and warehousing, courier and express delivery, and customs clearance in Dubai — ensuring your cargo reaches anywhere, safely and on time.
+ Q2. How can I get a quote for shipping in the UAE or globally?
A. You can easily request a quote through our website or by contacting our team. Provide shipment details such as cargo type, weight, origin, and destination, and we’ll offer a cost-effective plan. GulfBay Logistics ensures affordable freight forwarding in the UAE with transparent pricing and quick support.
+ Q3. What documents are required for international shipping from Dubai?
A. For most UAE export and import shipments, you’ll need a Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading or Airway Bill, Certificate of Origin, and sometimes additional permits. Our customs clearance experts in Dubai guide you through every step to ensure compliance and smooth clearance.
+ Q4. How long does customs clearance take in the UAE?
A. With GulfBay Logistics’ efficient customs brokerage services, most shipments clear within hours or up to one day if documents are complete. Complex or regulated goods may take longer, but our experienced team works closely with UAE Customs to minimize delays.
+ Q5. Does GulfBay Logistics provide door-to-door delivery?
A. Yes. We offer door-to-door freight forwarding services in Dubai and the UAE, covering pickup, packing, customs, and delivery. Whether domestic or international, our team ensures a smooth end-to-end logistics experience.
+ Q6. How are freight charges calculated?
A. Freight rates depend on cargo size, mode of transport (air, sea, or land), route, and delivery terms. As a trusted 3PL logistics company in the UAE, GulfBay Logistics provides competitive, transparent rates and helps you select the most efficient shipping option.
+ Q7. Does GulfBay Logistics offer insurance for shipments?
A. Yes, we provide cargo insurance services to protect your goods against damage, loss, or delay during transit. We partner with leading UAE insurers to ensure comprehensive coverage at the best value.
+ Q8. What warehousing solutions do you offer in the UAE?
A. Our 3PL warehousing services in Dubai include storage, inventory control, order fulfillment, packaging, and eCommerce logistics. GulfBay Logistics offers both short-term and long-term warehouse storage in the UAE, with secure facilities and value-added services.
+ Q9. Can GulfBay Logistics handle oversized or project cargo?
A. Absolutely. We specialize in project cargo logistics in Dubai, handling heavy machinery, construction materials, and oversized shipments with expert route planning, permits, and equipment. Safety and precision are at the heart of every project we manage.
+ Q10. How can I track my shipment?
A. Clients can track their shipments in real time via our freight tracking system. GulfBay Logistics provides shipment updates at each stage — pickup, transit, customs, and final delivery — for total visibility and peace of mind.
+ Q11. What are UAE Free Zones, and how can they benefit my business?
A. UAE Free Zones like JAFZA, DMCC, and DWC offer customs and tax advantages for import/export companies. As a trusted freight forwarder in Jebel Ali, GulfBay Logistics helps clients optimize their supply chains using free zone logistics and warehousing benefits.
